# DEV-1b-001 — Withdrawal of NDVI → LAI → N_l prior

**Date:** April 2026
**Status:** Resolved pre-sign-off (G2)
**Specification version affected:** Phase 1b SPEC.md v0.1-draft → v0.1
**Companion documents updated in parallel:** `vantage-mimics-litreview-v1_0_2.html` Decision 5

---

## Trigger

Citation audit during Phase 1b pre-sign-off review of the Sentinel-2 prior implementation in SPEC.md §5.

## Finding

The v0.1-draft specification placed two Sentinel-2-derived priors on physics-branch parameters:

1. **NDVI → LAI → N_l prior** (citing Lees et al. 2021)
2. **NDWI → m_g prior**

On audit, Lees et al. (2021) "Using remote sensing to assess peatland resilience by estimating soil surface moisture and drought recovery" (*Sci. Total Environ.* 761, 143312) was found to concern Sentinel-1 SAR backscatter as a proxy for water table depth in UK peatlands. It contains no NDVI → LAI relationship for heather. The cited Lees PhD thesis (Reading, 2019, "Measuring peatland carbon uptake by remote sensing") was checked as an alternative source; its optical-vegetation work is *Sphagnum*-focused and concerns GPP rather than leaf area, and it explicitly notes that NDVI → LAI for *Sphagnum* systems is poorly defined owing to ill-defined Beer's-law extinction coefficients (citing Weston et al. 2015).

The wider peatland-vegetation literature treats NDVI → LAI for mixed *Calluna–Sphagnum–Eriophorum* canopies as poorly constrained — NDVI saturates rapidly across the LAI ≈ 2–5 range relevant at Moor House, and chlorophyll-based indices (CI, MTCI) outperform NDVI as canopy-property predictors in these systems (Harris 2008; Lees et al. 2020; Letendre et al. 2008; Tucker et al. 2022).

A generic shrubland NDVI → LAI exponential with default *k_ext* = 0.5 was considered as a substitute and rejected: it would inject mis-calibrated noise into the prior rather than informative signal.

## Resolution

The N_l prior is withdrawn. The NDWI → m_g prior is retained — it concerns canopy water content, a different physical quantity with its own well-established physics, and is unaffected by the citation issue.

## Pre-registered v0.2 fallback

If post-experiment Diagnostic B (parameter correlation matrix) shows N_l non-identifiability, the documented v0.2 substitution is to introduce a chlorophyll-index-based prior (CI rather than NDVI) on N_l. **Reverting to the original NDVI → LAI chain is not the fallback.** This commitment is part of the pre-registration.
